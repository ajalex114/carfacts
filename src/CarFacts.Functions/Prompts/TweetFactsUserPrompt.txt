- Each tweet must be ≤260 characters (to leave room for 2-3 hashtags)

- Each fact should be from a DIFFERENT era and about a DIFFERENT brand/topic
- Include specific details: brand, model, year, what happened
- Tone: "Wait, did you know..." or "TIL that..." or just stating the fact with genuine surprise
- Do NOT use "You won't believe" or "SHOCKING" or any try-hard engagement bait
- Facts should stand alone — no references to a blog post or website

- "🚗 INCREDIBLE: The car that changed EVERYTHING! #MustRead"
