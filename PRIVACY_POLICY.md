# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 14, 2026**

## Introduction

Car Facts Daily ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website [carfactsdaily.com](https://carfactsdaily.com) and associated social media accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or interact with our content.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, the following information may be collected automatically by our hosting provider (WordPress.com):

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring URL
- Approximate geographic location (country/region level)

### Cookies

Our website is hosted on WordPress.com, which may use cookies and similar technologies for:

- Site analytics and performance monitoring
- Remembering user preferences
- Serving relevant content

We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites. For details on WordPress.com's cookie practices, please refer to the [Automattic Privacy Policy](https://automattic.com/privacy/).

## Information We Do Not Collect

- We do **not** require user registration or accounts.
- We do **not** collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or payment details.
- We do **not** sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## How We Use Information

Any automatically collected data is used solely to:

- Monitor website performance and availability
- Understand general audience demographics and interests
- Improve content quality and user experience
