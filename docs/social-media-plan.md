# CAR FACTS DAILY — SIMPLE SOCIAL MEDIA PLAN (NO TWITTER)

**Goal:** Grow traffic + brand visibility using free platforms only  
**Method:** One content engine → distributed everywhere  
**Focus:** Do ONE platform at a time, in order

---

## ✅ PHASE 1 — Instagram Reels (Start Here)
Instagram is your fastest free growth engine.

**What you do:**
- Create 1 reel per day
- Use 10–20 sec vertical video
- Use one car fact per reel
- Add text overlay + background image
- Schedule using Meta Business Suite (free)

**Why this is first:**
- Highest reach
- Zero cost
- Easy to repurpose
- Works perfectly with your car facts niche

> Do ONLY Instagram for the first 7 days.

---

## ✅ PHASE 2 — YouTube Shorts (Start after Instagram feels easy)
Use the same video you made for Instagram.

**What you do:**
- Upload 1 short per day
- Add a short caption
- Add 3–5 hashtags (#cars #ev #carfacts)

**Why this is second:**
- Shorts algorithm boosts new creators
- No extra work (same video as Instagram)

---

## ✅ PHASE 3 — Facebook Page
Facebook still gives strong organic reach for car content.

**What you do:**
- Auto-share your Instagram Reels to Facebook
- Post 1 image post per day (car fact)

**Why this is third:**
- Zero extra effort
- Meta Business Suite handles everything
- Reels perform well on Facebook too

---

## ✅ PHASE 4 — Pinterest (Long-term traffic)
Pinterest is a visual search engine — perfect for car facts.

**What you do:**
- Create 1 pin per day
- Use the same image you used for your reel
- Link the pin to your article
- Schedule inside Pinterest (free)

**Why this is fourth:**
- Builds long-term traffic
- Pins rank for months or years
- Easy to repurpose your existing content

---

## ✅ PHASE 5 — LinkedIn (Optional but powerful for EV + tech)
Use this only when you have extra time.

**What you do:**
- Post 3 times per week
- Focus on:
  - EV facts
  - Safety innovations
  - Car tech
  - Industry insights

**Why this is optional:**
- Good reach
- But not essential for early growth
- Works best once you have momentum
